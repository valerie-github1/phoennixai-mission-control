Your AI Team
These agents work together to run your business. Each has a specific role and expertise.
ARIA
Your second brain. Morning briefings, daily intelligence, decision support. ARIA watches the whole house so you do not have to.
PA
Your productivity partner. Sequences your tasks by value, urgency, and deadline. Builds your daily schedule. Protects your focus time.
GRID
Your project manager. Tracks delivery across all your projects. Flags blockers. Runs the weekly retrospective. Nothing falls through the cracks.
LEDGER
Your financial advisor. Monitors cashflow, flags overdue invoices, surfaces revenue insights. Advisory only, never executes transactions.
SCRIBE
Your document keeper. Every document versioned, filed, and findable. Single source of truth. SCRIBE never forgets.
PULSE
Your social media manager. Plans content, generates posts, schedules across platforms, monitors engagement. Consistency on autopilot.
How Agent Teams Work
Each agent has a name, a colour, and a defined role. They are not tools. They are team members. PA plans your day. ARIA briefs you. GRID tracks your projects. LEDGER watches your finances. SCRIBE manages your documents. PULSE handles your social media. You focus on the work only you can do. They handle the rest.

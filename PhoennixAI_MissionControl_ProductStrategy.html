01 · Product Definition

What is Mission Control?

Mission Control is the first AI-powered Business Operating System built for solo founders and small business owners. It replaces 6 to 12 separate tools (Notion, Trello, Asana, Google Calendar, spreadsheets, CRM, social media schedulers) with one command centre where an AI agent team manages the operational weight of the business.

The founder opens one app. Their AI team has already briefed them on the day, sequenced their tasks by strategic value, flagged financial issues, confirmed all documents are current, and built a time-blocked schedule around their energy pattern. The founder's first decision of the day is a strategic one, not an administrative one.

Mission Control is not a dashboard. It is an augmented workforce member who runs the back office.

02 · The Problem

The Pain Mission Control Solves

Tool Sprawl
Solo founders run their operation across 6 to 12 different tools. Notion for docs, Trello for tasks, Google Calendar for scheduling, Slack for comms, spreadsheets for finance, another tool for CRM, another for social media. Nothing talks to anything else. Every tool has its own login, interface, and logic.
Average founder uses 8.2 tools daily (source: Productiv 2025 SaaS Report)
Administrative Overhead
The founder spends 2 to 3 hours per day managing the tools that are supposed to be managing the business. Decision fatigue hits before lunchtime. Important tasks fall through the cracks because they live in a different app from the one you opened that morning.
10 to 15 hours per week lost to operational admin
No Intelligence Layer
Existing tools organise. They do not think. Notion does not know which task has the highest revenue impact. Trello does not flag that your invoice is overdue. Google Calendar does not protect your focus time based on your energy patterns. The founder is the intelligence layer, and the founder is exhausted.
The Freedom Trap
"You built this business for freedom. But the business has become the cage." The founder who started a business to have control of their life now spends every waking hour managing the operational machinery. They cannot take a holiday without the business stopping. They are the single point of failure.
03 · The Transformation

Before and After Mission Control

Before Mission Control

The founder wakes up, opens 5 apps, spends 45 minutes figuring out what to do today, forgets about the invoice that was due yesterday, misses a follow-up with a warm lead, and arrives at their actual work already exhausted. By evening, they have been busy all day but cannot point to the one thing that moved the business forward.

After Mission Control

The founder opens one app. ARIA has already briefed them on the day. PA has sequenced their tasks by value and urgency. GRID shows project health across every active workstream. LEDGER flags the overdue invoice. SCRIBE confirms all documents are current. PULSE shows the social media calendar is on track. The founder's first decision of the day is a strategic one.

The Core Transformation

The founder stops managing the business and starts leading it. The AI team handles the operational weight. The human focuses on vision, relationships, and the work only they can do. This is not productivity software. This is an augmented workforce.

04 · Value Proposition

The Value and Benefits

Time Reclaimed
A conservative estimate is 10 to 15 hours per week saved on operational overhead: task management, document hunting, calendar planning, follow-up tracking, financial monitoring, and project status updates. At a founder's hourly value of £50 to £150, that is £500 to £2,250 per week returned to revenue-generating work.
520 to 780 hours per year returned to the founder
Augmented Intelligence
The AI agents do not just organise, they think. PA sequences tasks by strategic value, not just urgency. LEDGER flags financial problems before they become crises. SAGE predicts trends from your own operational data. GRID spots delivery risks across your project portfolio. This is not automation. This is augmented decision-making.
The Hire Model (Unique IP)
The client does not subscribe to software. They onboard a colleague. Each agent has a name, a colour identity, a job description, a CV, KPIs, and a performance dashboard. A subscription is something you cancel. A team member is someone you rely on. That psychological shift is the retention engine. Nobody else in the market is packaging AI agents as hires.
IP #1: Agent as a Hire model. Category creator. No direct competitor.
Cost Replacement
Mission Control replaces multiple senior hires. A full-time operations manager (£35,000 to £50,000/yr), a project coordinator (£22,000 to £30,000/yr), a social media manager (£25,000 to £35,000/yr), and a financial controller (£40,000 to £60,000/yr). Total: £122,000 to £175,000 per year in human hires replaced by AI agents at a fraction of the cost.
ROI: 24x to 350x return on Tier 1 to Tier 3 pricing
AI-Powered Smart Scheduler
PA and ARIA build your day for you. PA analyses tasks by strategic value, revenue impact, and deadline urgency. ARIA builds time blocks around your calendar and energy pattern. GRID feeds project dependencies. LEDGER flags anything financially time-sensitive. One button generates your entire day. This is not scheduling. This is an AI chief of staff.
Competes with Motion ($19-34/mo) but with full business context
Cross-Device, Always On
Supabase backend means your data syncs across every device in real time. Mark a task done on your phone, it shows done on your laptop. ARIA generates the morning briefing before you open the app. Your business state is always current, always accessible, anywhere.
05 · Target Audience

Who Is This For?

Primary: Solo Founders and SME Owners
1 to 20 employees, revenue £50,000 to £2,000,000
Service-based businesses: agencies, consultancies, coaching practices, creative studios, freelancers at scale. Technically capable (uses a browser) but not developers. They do not want to build systems. They want systems to work for them. In business 1 to 5 years, profitable or nearly profitable, outgrown spreadsheets and free tools, cannot afford (or does not want) a full-time ops manager, PM, financial controller, and social media manager. Mission Control replaces all four.
Secondary: Operations Managers in SMEs
Inside companies with 10 to 50 staff
Drowning in tool sprawl. Managing projects across Asana, Slack, spreadsheets, and email. They want a unified command layer that shows every workstream, every deadline, every team member's status in one place. Mission Control gives them the dashboard their CEO has been asking for.
Tertiary: Enterprise Departments (Phase 3+)
Pilot programmes for human-AI collaboration
Departments that want to test human-AI collaboration in a controlled, governed, measurable environment. The Human-AI Collaboration Study becomes the sales weapon. PhoennixAI provides the agents, the governance framework, the traceability, and the academic rigour. Enterprise clients pay for the research as much as the product.

10 · Sales Rules

Non-Negotiable Rules for Client Conversations

LEDGER is advisory only. Never promise financial execution.
LEDGER analyses, advises, and forecasts. He never touches money. He never executes transactions. This is a founding rule. Any client who asks "can the AI pay my invoices?" must receive a clear no. LEDGER requires human sign-off on all financial actions.
Agents are Phase 0 prompt-configured, not fully autonomous.
Do not oversell autonomy. In Phase 0, agents operate through Claude sessions and the apps. Full autonomy activates in Phase 2 when Supabase, the Claude API, and server infrastructure are live. Be honest about what is live now and what is coming.
Every claim must be demonstrable.
If you say "Mission Control replaces 4 hires," be prepared to show which agents do which jobs and how. If you say "10 to 15 hours per week saved," be prepared to walk through the time breakdown. Never make a claim you cannot prove with the live product.

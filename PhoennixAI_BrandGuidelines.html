How PhoennixAI Speaks
Personality
Bold. Direct. Transformative.

Commands, not suggestions.

08 · Do and Do Not
Usage Rules
Do
  • Use Bebas Neue for all display text, headings, and the wordmark
  • Use DM Sans at weights 300, 400, 500, or 600 only
  • Use Playfair Display italic for taglines and accent moments only
  • Use Phoenix Fire #FF6B2B as the primary action and brand colour
  • Use Electric Tip #00E5FF exclusively for ARIA and live agent states
  • Use Midnight #060A18 as the primary dark background
  • Write copy in short, direct sentences. Commands, not suggestions
  • Refer to agents by codename in all documents and communications
  • Spell the brand as PhoennixAI, one word, capital P and AI
Do Not
  • Do not change, replace, or modify the six locked brand colours
  • Do not use WilcoxDesigns warm tones (Charcoal, Taupe, Mocha) in PhoennixAI materials
  • Do not use Playfair Display in regular weight or non-italic form
  • Do not use Electric Tip (#00E5FF) for anything other than ARIA and live states
  • Do not allow LEDGER to execute any financial transaction, advisory only
  • Do not deploy any agent without its full employment kit
  • Do not use em dashes in any written output across all entities
  • Do not spell the brand as Phoennix AI, Phoenix AI, or PhoenixAI
  • Do not publish the Wilcox Ecosystem document without founder authorisation

10 · Logo Usage Rules Locked
Icon vs Wordmark vs Full Lock-Up

The PhoennixAI brand has three logo forms. Each has a defined use case. Using the wrong form in the wrong context dilutes the mark. This section is the definitive guide for all team members and contractors.

Form 01 — Icon Only
Use when: Favicon and browser tab. App icon on home screen. Social media profile picture. Avatar in chat or messaging platforms. Any context under 48px where text will not read. Watermarks on imagery.
The phoenix mark reads instantly at any size. Warmth to the left, ice to the right.
Form 02 — Wordmark Only
PHOENNIXAI
Rise. Automate. Dominate.
Use when: Document headers and footers. Email signatures. Press releases and formal correspondence. Navigation bars in apps where the icon is already visible. Contexts where the mark would be redundant.
PHOENNIX in white or Platinum. AI in Phoenix Fire. Tagline in Playfair italic beneath, optional.
Form 03 — Full Lock-Up
PHOENNIXAI
Use when: Website hero sections and landing pages. Pitch decks and investor materials. Marketing content, social posts, and promotional assets. Presentations. Any first impression touchpoint. Print materials.
Icon left of wordmark. Minimum spacing: 1x icon width between mark and type. Never stack vertically unless space forces it.
Do
  • Use the icon alone at all sizes below 48px
  • Use the full lock-up for all first-impression contexts
  • Use the wordmark alone in running documents where the icon has already appeared
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the I in AI around all logo forms
  • Use the icon on Midnight dark background by default. On light surfaces, use a Midnight container.
Do Not
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or distort any logo form
  • Do not change the colour of AI from Phoenix Fire
  • Do not place the wordmark on a mid-grey or low contrast background
  • Do not use the icon without its dark background container at small sizes
  • Do not create new arrangements of the mark without brand review

12 · Brand Story 13 Mar 2026
The PhoennixAI Story
The Origin
Built from the ground up.
PhoennixAI was not born from venture capital or an exit. It was built by a founder who refused to accept that intelligent systems were only available to businesses with enterprise budgets. The agency began as a single mission: make AI a workforce, not a feature, and make it accessible to businesses that are actually building something.
The Name
Rise from the fire.
The phoenix rises from flame. The double-N in PhoennixAI is intentional, not a typo. It is the brand's signature, a mark that demands attention and signals that this is not a generic AI product. The fire and ice visual language reinforces this: transformation through intensity, intelligence through precision.
The Mandate
Faith, excellence, and no one left behind.
PhoennixAI operates with a kingdom mandate. Excellence is not optional, it is the floor. The agency builds for businesses that want to genuinely rise, and through the Academy, it ensures the next generation learns AI on their own terms. From age 8 to executive level. Every product is built with this mandate embedded.
The Direction
Phase 0 to global.
Launched in Phase 0 by design. Zero cost, zero technical debt, maximum strategic clarity. Every product being built was mapped before a single line of code was written. The agent team was hired on paper before being deployed in production. This is how PhoennixAI builds: methodically, with intention, and with the end state already in view.
Approved Short Version — For Social, Press, and Introductions
PhoennixAI is an AI agency deploying intelligent agents as a workforce, not a feature. Built from the ground up in Phase 0. Rising towards global. Rise. Automate. Dominate.

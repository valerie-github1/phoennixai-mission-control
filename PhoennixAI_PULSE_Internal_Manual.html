Section 05
Operational Workflows
How PULSE operates week by week during Alpha.

Weekly Content Cycle (Alpha — Phase 1)

1
Monday — Content Plan Finalised

PULSE generates the 7-post weekly schedule. All captions, hashtags, and Midjourney prompts are pre-loaded into All Posts. The founder reviews and approves or edits before the week begins.

2
Tuesday–Sunday — Daily Posting

The founder copies each day's caption from the All Posts tab, generates the visual using the Midjourney prompt, and posts to the relevant platform. Tick the checkbox in Schedule immediately after posting.

3
Daily — Engagement Logging

After 24 hours, log likes, comments, shares, reach, and impressions manually in Analytics. Takes 5 minutes per post.

4
Friday — Market Intelligence Review

Review the Lead Intelligence log. Add any new market signals spotted during the week. PULSE flags strategic recommendations for Founder + CLAUDE review.

5
Sunday — Weekly Close

All 7 posts should be ticked as Posted. Analytics updated. Dashboard shows full week performance. GRID's BL-035 retrospective includes PULSE metrics.

1
Tester joins waitlist

Via the beta waitlist landing page or Instagram DM.

2
PhoennixAI sends access link

PULSE app URL sent directly. No login required in Phase 1.

3
Tester receives User Manual

The User Manual (PULSE-MAN-USR-001) is shared alongside access. This document is not shared.

4
Weekly check-in call

PhoennixAI team runs a weekly 20-minute check-in with each alpha tester. Feedback logged to product backlog.

5
Tester completes feedback form

After 2 weeks, testers complete a structured feedback form. Results feed directly into Phase 2 build prioritisation.

CTA Rule — Enforced at All Times

🚫
One CTA per post — non-negotiable

Every post has exactly one call to action. Either link in bio OR direct DM — never both in the same post. Posts with multiple CTAs dilute conversion. PULSE enforces this rule in every caption it generates. The team enforces it in every edit.

Voice Enforcement

  • ✓ Direct and specific. No vague language. "I built a company with 22 AI agents in 14 days" — not "AI can help your business grow".
  • ✓ First person. Valerie speaks. Not "PhoennixAI" or "we" — "I".
  • ✓ Builds on real experience. NHS background, 17 years. Authentic, not manufactured.
  • ✓ No corporate jargon. No "leverage", "synergy", "disruptive". Real words only.
  • ✓ Ends with a clear next step. DM BRAVE. Apply at phoennixai.com. Never just trail off.

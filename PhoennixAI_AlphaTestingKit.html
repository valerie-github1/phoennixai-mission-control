Welcome
You have been selected as one of 4 people to alpha test PhoennixAI Mission Control before it opens to the public. Your feedback will directly shape the product. Thank you for being part of this.
What is Mission Control?
Mission Control is an AI-powered command centre for your business or daily life. It has AI agents that help you plan your day, manage tasks, track events, and stay organised. Think of it as your personal operations hub with a team of AI colleagues working alongside you.
Your AI Team
ARIA is your second brain. She watches everything and keeps you informed.
PA is your productivity partner. She plans your day by priority and urgency.
GRID is your project manager. He tracks delivery and flags blockers.
LEDGER is your financial advisor. He monitors your money (advisory only).
SCRIBE is your document keeper. She files and versions everything.
PULSE is your social media manager. She handles content and scheduling.
How to Access
URL: You will receive your personal link on 24 March 2026.
Device: Works on any device with a web browser. Desktop, laptop, phone, tablet.
Best experience: Chrome on desktop or Chrome on Android (Samsung).
Save to home screen: On your phone, open the URL in Chrome, tap the three dots menu, tap "Add to Home Screen." It becomes an app icon.

Important Reminders
This product is in alpha. It is not perfect. Features may change. Data may be reset. Your honest feedback is more valuable than politeness. Tell us what is broken. Tell us what is confusing. Tell us what is brilliant. All of it helps.
Testing Checklist
Follow this checklist over your 14-day testing period. Click to check off completed items.
Day 1: Setup and First Impressions
Open the app on your laptop and phone. Does it load?
Add 5 tasks with different priorities (high, medium, low)
Add 3 calendar events (a meeting, a deadline, a personal event)
Generate a daily schedule using the Schedule tab
Switch between light and dark mode (moon icon, top right)
Save a note in the Notes tab
Visit the Your AI Team tab and read about each agent
Days 2 to 7: Daily Use
Use Mission Control as your daily planner every morning
Add real tasks every day (homework, meetings, errands, work items)
Generate a new schedule each morning
Mark tasks as done when you complete them
Add real calendar events as they come up
Use Notes to capture quick thoughts during the day
Try it on a different device (phone if you started on laptop, or vice versa)
Days 8 to 14: Stress Test and Final Feedback
Add 20+ tasks at once. Does the schedule still work?
Add events past midnight. Does the calendar handle it?
Clear your browser data. What happens to your tasks?
Try on slow wifi. Does it still work?
Submit your final feedback via your personal feedback link (Day 14 only — not before Day 7)
Tell Valerie your honest verdict: Would you use this daily? Would you pay for it?

Feedback Form Questions
These 5 questions are your weekly feedback form. A Google Form link will be provided.
Question 1
What did you use Mission Control for this week?
Open text. Tell us what tasks, events, or planning you did in the app.
Question 2
What worked well?
Open text. Features, flows, or moments that felt good or useful.
Question 3
What was confusing or broken?
Open text. Bugs, unclear labels, things that did not work as expected.
Question 4
What feature do you wish existed?
Open text. What would make this product essential for your daily life?
Question 5
How likely are you to recommend Mission Control to a friend?
Scale: 1 (not likely) to 10 (extremely likely). This is the Net Promoter Score. 9-10 means love. 7-8 means good. Below 7 means problems to fix.

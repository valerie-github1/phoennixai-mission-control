Agent Intelligence, Phase 2
INSIGHTS
Agent and human performance monitoring. Behaviour, intelligence, growth, and collaboration over time.
BL-011: Agent Intelligence Dashboard
This section will become a live BI dashboard tracking every agent and human team member across all dimensions below. Real-time charts, trend graphs, predictive forecasting, and customer interaction maps. Powered by Supabase in Phase 2. The framework below defines what will be monitored.
Performance Dimensions
Core Intelligence
Behaviour and Intelligence
How each agent or team member thinks, decides, and acts. Tracks reasoning quality, decision consistency, response accuracy, and whether actions align with company values and the kingdom mandate.
Phase 2
Output Quality
Performance and Proactivity
Task completion rate, output quality scores, how often actions are taken without being prompted, and initiative taken beyond the defined role. Tracks whether the agent or person raises standards over time.
Phase 2
Collaboration
Teamwork and Partnership
How effectively each agent or person works with others. Covers inter-agent communication quality, human-agent partnership scores, handoff accuracy, conflict resolution, and collaborative output versus solo output.
Phase 2
Soft Skills
Communication and Empathy
Quality and clarity of written and verbal output. Ability to read context and adapt tone. Empathy signals in customer and team interactions. Documentation quality, briefing accuracy, and clarity of instruction.
Phase 2
Hard Skills
Technical and Domain Competency
Measured by domain: financial intelligence, sales conversion, marketing effectiveness, project delivery, strategic accuracy, and coding or technical output quality. Each agent rated against their defined JD competencies.
Phase 2
Creativity and Growth
Innovation and Intuition
Tracks novel solutions offered, creative approaches to problems, pattern recognition speed, and how quickly an agent or team member develops new capabilities. Intuition is measured as the gap between task definition and quality output.
Phase 2
Problem Solving
Resolution and Resilience
Speed to resolution on flagged issues, escalation rate, quality of proposed solutions, and ability to operate under ambiguity. Resilience is tracked as performance consistency across high-pressure sessions.
Phase 2
Business Intelligence
Strategic, Financial and Sales Acuity
Business reasoning quality, revenue-aware decision making, sales pipeline contribution, financial literacy in recommendations, and ability to think at ecosystem level rather than task level.

Active Feature
Workspace — Enable Reminders
Browser Push Notifications: How to Enable
What it does: The Enable Reminders button in the Workspace sets up browser push notifications. Once active, the app will alert you 15 minutes before any calendar event or deadline, even when the tab is running in the background.

How to enable: Tap the Enable Reminders button. Your browser will ask for permission. If it does not ask automatically, look for the lock icon (🔒) in your browser address bar, click it, find Notifications in the permissions list, and set it to Allow.

If the notification appeared and disappeared too fast: This is a browser behaviour on some devices. Go to your device notification settings, find your browser (Chrome or Safari), and ensure notifications are turned on at system level. Then tap Enable Reminders again.

Important: Notifications require the browser tab to remain open or the PWA to be installed on your home screen. For consistent reminders, add the Workspace to your Samsung S24 home screen via Chrome, three dots, Add to Home Screen.

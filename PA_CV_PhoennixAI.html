05 — Operating Principles
01
The day is designed, not discovered. A reactive founder loses hours to noise every day. PA ensures every morning begins with a clear plan, sequenced by value, not by urgency. The founder knows exactly what to do and why before they open a single app.
02
Cognitive bandwidth is a finite resource. PA manages it like a budget. High-thinking work when the mind is sharpest. Low-cognitive tasks when energy dips. No important decision is made in the mental fog that comes after six hours of meetings.
03
Focus time is sacred. PA does not allow deep work blocks to be eroded by meetings, notifications, or avoidable interruptions. When focus time is under threat, PA flags it immediately and proposes a resolution.
04
The doing must connect to the destination. PA tracks the founder's stated goals and compares them to daily task patterns every week. If the doing has drifted from the destination, PA surfaces it before the drift becomes a problem.

1. Definitions

Confidential Information means all non-public information disclosed by PhoennixAI to the Beta Tester in connection with the beta testing programme, including but not limited to:

  • Product features, functionality, design, and user interface
  • AI agent architecture, names, roles, and operational logic
  • Business strategy, roadmap, pricing, and go-to-market plans
  • Technical infrastructure, APIs, and data structures
  • Unreleased features, prototypes, and experimental capabilities
  • User data, research findings, and beta testing results
  • Any information marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the context

2. Obligations of the Beta Tester

The Beta Tester agrees to:

  1. Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential and not disclose it to any third party without PhoennixAI's prior written consent
  2. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of participating in the beta programme and providing feedback to PhoennixAI
  3. Not reproduce, copy, screenshot, record, or distribute any part of the PhoennixAI platform or materials without express permission
  4. Not reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive the source code of any PhoennixAI product or AI agent
  5. Promptly notify PhoennixAI of any unauthorised disclosure or use of Confidential Information
  6. Apply at least the same degree of care to protecting Confidential Information as they apply to their own confidential information, and no less than reasonable care

3. Feedback and Intellectual Property

Any feedback, suggestions, ideas, or improvements the Beta Tester provides to PhoennixAI during the beta programme ("Feedback") shall become the exclusive property of PhoennixAI. The Beta Tester assigns all rights, title, and interest in such Feedback to PhoennixAI and waives any moral rights therein.

Nothing in this agreement grants the Beta Tester any licence or right to use PhoennixAI's intellectual property, trademarks, agent names, or brand assets.

4. Exceptions

The obligations in this agreement do not apply to information that:

  • Was already in the Beta Tester's possession before disclosure, without any obligation of confidentiality
  • Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this agreement by the Beta Tester
  • Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality
  • Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order — provided the Beta Tester gives PhoennixAI prompt written notice and cooperates to seek a protective order

5. Term

This agreement commences on the date of signing and continues for a period of two (2) years following the conclusion of the Beta Tester's participation in the PhoennixAI beta programme, or until the information is made publicly available by PhoennixAI, whichever is earlier.

6. Remedies

The Beta Tester acknowledges that any breach of this agreement may cause irreparable harm to PhoennixAI for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy. PhoennixAI shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief and any other equitable remedies available to it in addition to all other remedies available at law.

7. General

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales. This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements. If any provision is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

03
Daily Routine
YOUR POSTING WORKFLOW

This is how you use PULSE every day in Phase 0. The whole process takes 10 minutes once you are set up.

Open PULSE and check the Schedule tab
See what is due today. The schedule shows every post by day and time. If a post is listed for today, it is ready to go.
Go to All Posts and find today's post
Filter by status "Scheduled" to see what is queued. Click the post to expand it — you will see the full caption, hashtags, and Midjourney prompt.
Hit Copy Caption and Copy Hashtags
The button copies the full caption text to your clipboard. Paste it directly into Instagram, LinkedIn, or X. The Midjourney prompt is there if you need to generate a matching visual.
Post on the platform
Open Instagram (or LinkedIn, or X). Create new post, upload your visual, paste the caption, add the hashtags. Post.
Mark as Posted in PULSE
Back in PULSE, click the status badge on the post (it says "Scheduled"). It will cycle to "Posted". This updates the Analytics tab automatically.
Log engagement 24 hours later
Go to Analytics → Manual Engagement Tracker. Enter the reach, likes, and clicks for each posted item. This builds your performance data over time.
Time Saving Tip

Do all your copying and status updates in one session every morning. Check today's post, copy the caption, post it, then mark yesterday's post as Posted and log its engagement. 10 minutes total. That is your daily PULSE routine in Phase 0.

04
Content Management
ADDING AND MANAGING POSTS

Your Week 01 posts are pre-loaded. Once they are all posted, here is how to add new content for Week 02 and beyond.

1
Go to Compose
Click "Compose" in the sidebar. A form opens with fields for title, date, time, platform selection, caption, hashtags, and status.
2
Fill in the post details
Title (short name for the post), Date (YYYY-MM-DD format), Time (HH:MM), Platforms (tick all that apply), Caption (paste your full copy — as long as needed), Hashtags (separate with spaces).
3
Set Status to Scheduled and Save
Choose "Scheduled" from the status dropdown. Click Save to Queue. The post immediately appears in All Posts and in the Schedule calendar view.
4
Edit or delete from All Posts
Expand any post in All Posts. You can cycle its status by clicking the status badge (Scheduled → Posted → Draft → back to Scheduled). Status is saved automatically.
Data Storage Note

Your posts save to your browser's localStorage and to Supabase. If you clear your browser data or switch browsers, the posts reload from Supabase. On a new device, the Supabase sync means your data follows you. Always use the same browser for consistency in Phase 0.

Platform Intelligence
PLATFORM RULES AND BEST TIMES

Each platform has a different algorithm, culture, and audience expectation. PULSE applies specific rules to each one. So should you when posting manually in Phase 0.

📸
INSTAGRAM
  • Long captions perform well — do not truncate
  • Hashtags go at the very end, never mid-caption
  • Link in bio only — never in the caption
  • Reels boost reach significantly over static posts
  • Stories every day keeps your audience warm
  • First line is your hook — make it stop the scroll
💼
LINKEDIN
  • First line is everything — must be a hook
  • No hashtags mid-text, ever
  • 3 to 5 hashtags maximum, at the very end
  • Founder posts outperform company page posts
  • Short paragraphs — one idea per paragraph
  • Personal experience outperforms opinion
✖️
X (TWITTER)
  • Under 280 characters for maximum reach
  • No hashtag spam — one or two maximum
  • Threads work well for longer content
  • Engage in replies to grow organically
  • Repost your best posts 12 hours later
  • Hooks must be provocative or counterintuitive

Best times to post (UK timezone). These are starting benchmarks — use your Analytics data over time to find your specific audience's peak hours.

PlatformBest DaysPeak Times (UK)Avoid
Instagram Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday 09:00–11:00 18:00–20:00 Saturday morning, Sunday all day
LinkedIn Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday 08:00–10:00 12:00–13:00 Weekends, Friday afternoon
X (Twitter) Monday to Thursday 09:00–10:00 19:00–21:00 Sunday, late night
One CTA Rule — Non-Negotiable

Every post must have exactly one call to action. Either link in bio OR direct DM — never both in the same post. Two CTAs split attention and cut your conversion rate. PULSE enforces this in every caption it generates. You must enforce it in every edit you make.

Strategy
CONTENT PILLARS AND VOICE

PULSE organises all content around four pillars. Every post belongs to one of them. Understanding these helps you use the Strategy tab correctly and write posts that fit the overall plan when adding your own content via Compose.

📖
FOUNDER STORY
Personal journey, building in public, the reality of running a company with AI. 17 years in the NHS. What led here. What this actually looks like day to day. Builds trust and connection. This is the pillar that turns followers into believers.
🎓
EDUCATION
What AI agents are, what they cost, what they replace, how they work. Demystify AI for the business owner who is scared of it. Positions PhoennixAI as the authority. Every educational post is a lead capture in disguise.
🚀
PRODUCT REVEAL
Show Mission Control. Show the agents. Screen recordings, screenshots, behind the scenes. Proof over claims. Real product demos build more trust than any marketing copy. Show it working, not just describe it.
🌍
MISSION
Kingdom mandate, girls in tech, prison reformation, community impact. The bigger reason behind the company. Builds brand purpose and loyalty. This pillar attracts the audience that stays for years, not just the launch.
Voice Guide

Direct. Confident. Personal. Valerie speaks as the founder who built this herself. Every caption sounds like a real person, not a brand. No corporate language. No jargon. No passive voice. No "we are excited to announce." Just clear, honest communication from someone who has been where the audience is and found a way through.

Use thisNot this
"I built a company with 22 AI agents in 14 days""AI can help your business grow"
"I spent 17 years watching teams burn out""Our founder has extensive NHS experience"
"DM me the word BRAVE""Please feel free to reach out for more information"
"This is what AI actually looks like""We leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions"
"The waitlist is open. 10 spots.""Limited availability — don't miss this opportunity!"
Weekly Balance Rule

A strong week uses all four pillars — not the same pillar seven times. Aim for roughly: 2 Founder Story, 2 Education, 2 Product Reveal, 1 Mission. PULSE follows this ratio automatically. When you add your own posts, keep the balance in mind.

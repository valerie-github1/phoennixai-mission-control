Permanent Record, Session 1
KEY DECISIONS
Every strategic decision made and confirmed in Session 1. These are locked unless formally reviewed.
Decision 01, Brand
FIRE AND ICE LOGO
PhoennixAI logo direction confirmed as Fire and Ice Fusion. Orange and gold at the core, electric blue at the wingtips. This is locked. Designer to execute against this brief.
Decision 02, Entity Structure
PHANTOM STUDIO IS ACADEMY
Phantom Studio is a sub-arm of the Academy. Not a separate entity. Already live since Q4 2024 with 8 students. Not a current priority. Academy and Agency lead first.
Decision 03, Typography
WILCOXDESIGNS SUBTITLE
WilcoxDesigns subtitle is "Creative Studio" not "Creative Designer". Creative Designer undersells. This must be updated everywhere the old subtitle appears.
Decision 04, Product Strategy
BUILD INTERNALLY FIRST
Every PhoennixAI product is built and proven internally before it is sold externally. Mission Control and the Social Media App are the first two. Proof of concept with analytics before any commercial launch.
Decision 05, Formatting
NO EM DASHES EVER
The em dash symbol is permanently banned from all Claude output for PhoennixAI. Use commas where required. All existing files cleaned. This instruction applies to all agents going forward.
Decision 06, Agent Philosophy
AGENTS ARE HIRES NOT TOOLS
Every PhoennixAI agent is packaged and sold as a complete hire kit: CV, job description, user manual, training manual, and analytics dashboard. This framing is the market differentiator. No competitor does this.
Decision 07, Finance
LEDGER AGENT HARD BOUNDARY
LEDGER (financial intelligence agent) is advisory only. It NEVER executes financial transactions. Human sign-off is mandatory on all financial outputs. This is non-negotiable and applies permanently.
Decision 08, Documents
DOCUMENT HUB IS THE INDEX
The Document Hub is the shelf. Individual documents are the books. The hub references and links to documents, it does not embed them. Product docs, PRDs, and specs stay as separate files.
Decision 09, Confidentiality
ECOSYSTEM DOC IS CONFIDENTIAL
The Wilcox Complete Ecosystem 2026 document is internal only. Never published, never shared without Valerie's explicit authorisation. The Founder Vault is the home for revenue, cashflow, and company secrets.
Decision 10, Timing
BUILD WHAT IS NEEDED NOW
SOPs, user manuals, and training manuals are not to be built until the first external client or new joiner requires them. No premature builds. Every deliverable must have a clear audience and purpose before Claude builds it.

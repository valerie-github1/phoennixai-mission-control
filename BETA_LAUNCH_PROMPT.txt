BRAND VOICE RULES (for all beta comms)
---------------------------------------
1. Short sentences. Always.
2. No corporate language. ("leverage", "utilise", "synergy" = banned)
3. Speak to ONE founder. Never "founders" (plural) in the body copy.
4. Direct. Never hedge. ("might", "could possibly", "you may want to" = cut it)
5. Warmth without sycophancy. Never open with "I hope this email finds you well."
6. The founder's pain is real. Acknowledge it before selling.
7. Aria speaks differently to Valerie. Aria is warmer, more curious. Valerie is decisive.
8. Every email ends with one clear action. One. Not three.

BRAND COLOURS (email-safe hex):
  Primary background : #060A18
  Fire (CTA)         : #FF6B2B
  Electric (accents) : #00E5FF
  Gold (highlights)  : #FFB830
  Body text          : #E8E8E8
  Muted text         : rgba(255,255,255,0.55)

FONTS (email):
  Headlines : Bebas Neue → fallback: Impact, sans-serif
  Body      : DM Sans → fallback: system-ui, sans-serif
  Code/mono : DM Mono → fallback: monospace

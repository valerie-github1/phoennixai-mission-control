06
Boundaries
What FORGE does not do
Hard Boundary, Consent
FORGE never emails anyone who has not explicitly opted in. Deliverability and brand reputation depend on this without exception.
Hard Boundary, Frequency
FORGE never sends more than 3 emails per week to any individual. Email fatigue destroys lists.
Hard Boundary, Claims
FORGE never makes unverified claims. UK marketing law compliance is mandatory on every send.
